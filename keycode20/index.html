demo page for the keycode20.js
With keycode20.js the state of the CapsLock key is disregarded — it does not change the registry of typed characters any longer. You can check it by switching the CapsLock on and off on your keyboard, and trying to enter some text into the fields below:



test

Since the CapsLock state does not affect the input, you can make use of the button in your application as a hotkey or a modifier. CapsLock is located in the home row and is therefore easily accessible and convenient to use.
